COURSE

University of lagos has eight schools and thirty four academic departments with a total of sixty four accredited programmes, across ND, HND and post HND levels. The college also offers certificate courses.UNILAG offers B.Sc(Ed)courses in technical and vocational education and post graduate Diplomas in Engineering. The two programmes are run in conjunction with the UNIVERSITY of Nigeria ; Nsukka and the federal university of Technology, Akure, respectively. The student population which is made up of both full time and part time students is in the range of 15,000, while the staff strength is 16,000.
